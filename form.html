Custom Surfboards

Here at L T S C our surfboards are 100% handmade from start to finish in our workshop in Cornwall. This means that we can make your dream board a reality, whether its a classic high performance surfboard or something completely different. Don't worry if there's anything that you're unsure of, we're here to work with you every step of the way and fill in any missed details.

Due to the nature of our work and the materials used, resin colours and tints may vary slightly from board to board. Please provide as much detail as possible in the form below and if you have any image references for colour or design please also provide these.
Once we have recieved your order we will contact you confirming any details along with any questions we may have regarding your order.
