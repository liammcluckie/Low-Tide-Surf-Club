Handcrafted Surfboards on the North Coast of Cornwall

Ethos

Manufacturing surf craft is notoriously damaging to the environment. Thats why here at L T S C we produce high-quality sustainable surfboards that have the same technical performance attributes of any modern boards, while having reduced environmental and toxic impacts. This is possible from managing and maintaining a measurably reduced carbon footprint, using renewable and recycled materials, and processes that reduce toxicity during production.

Customer Service

We have a small, dedicated, and highly experienced customer service team who are all surfers just like you. Our team are here to help you every step of the way, their combined knowledge and experience of the surf industry and surfboard manufacturing make our custom board ordering a breeze. They'll get you out in the line-up on your fresh stick in no time!

Our promise

All of our orders come with a 28 day ride away satisfaction promise. This means that if within 28 days of recieving the board you're not entirely happy with the product it can be returned or exchanged. Other than our high quality craft one of the other many reasons that sets L T S C apart from other shapers is our Surfboard Aftercare; For the duration of the first year of purchase we will fix any damaged boards free of charge, you've just got to get it to us.
